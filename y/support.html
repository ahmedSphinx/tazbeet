Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new task?

Tap the + button on the home screen, fill in the task details, and save. You can add subtasks, set priorities, and assign categories.

How does the Pomodoro timer work?

Start a Pomodoro session from the timer screen. Work for the set duration, then take a break. Sessions automatically cycle through work and break periods.

Can I sync my data across devices?

Yes! Sign in with your account to sync tasks, categories, and settings across all your devices via Firebase.

How do I track my mood?

Go to the Mood section, tap the + button, and log your current energy, focus, and stress levels. View insights over time.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use Firebase for secure data storage and encryption. Your personal information is never shared without your consent.

Troubleshooting

Having issues? Try these common solutions:

  • Restart the app or your device
  • Check your internet connection for sync issues
  • Ensure you have the latest app version from the store
  • Clear app cache in settings if experiencing performance issues

If problems persist, please contact us with details about your device, app version, and the issue you're experiencing.
